About

Hey there! I'm Jasmine, a product UX designer and front-end developer.

As a kid, I loved creating art and playing video games. I studied graphic design in university because I thought that I would learn how to design games. From there, I discovered - and was captivated by - the practice of product (UX) design. It utilized my artistic skills, and I could design fun experiences within complex systems! After that, I started studying computer science and programming so I could learn how to develop better experiences too.

Although I mainly focus on product design and research, I like to be involved throughout the entire development lifecycle. I have helped manage projects, perform quality assurance testing, and even develop features. All of this has made me a better designer and makes me appreciate all of the work that goes into a product.

So - I still enjoy art and games. However, most of my current studies revolve around product design, game design, and programming. My other hobbies include learning Cantonese and Mandarin, reading, and cooking, but they still find their way into my design work somehow. :)

Now, how do you design less frustrating experiences with printers...?
